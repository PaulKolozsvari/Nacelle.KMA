Important information

  • Online check-in opens 24 hours before your flight and closes 1 hour prior to your departure.
  • A valid ID book, driver's licence or passport must be presented at check-in and the boarding gate.
  • Boarding passes can be accessed electronically through the kulula travel app or our self-service online check-in website. Boarding passes can also be printed at the self-service kiosk or obtained at check-in counters.
  • Minors younger than 18 years of age travelling to or from South Africa need to travel with the necessary documentation including an unabridged birth certificate.
  • Hand baggage allowance consists of one bag of 7kg (dimensions: 36 x 23 x 56cm, including handles, pockets and wheels) plus a slimline laptop bag or small hand bag. Should your hand baggage exceed these specifications, you will be sent back to check-in to purchase an extra 20kg bag at the airport.
  • Checked baggage allowance is one free bag no heavier than 20kg (dimensions: 75 x 43 x 90cm, including handles, pockets and wheels). Additional bags will be charged for at a per bag fee at the airport or can be purchased online up to 2 hours before departure at a reduced cost. Bags weighing between 20-32kg are subject to a heavy bag fee.
  • Your checked bags must be dropped off before airport check-in closes at least 45 minutes prior to departure for domestic flights and 60 minutes prior to departure for international flights.
  • Seats may change if there is an aircraft change.

Baggage security questions

Please review and confirm the below statements*

  • I'm travelling with my own luggage.
  • I've packed my own bags, no one else helped me pack or packed on my behalf.
  • If I'm carrying something on behalf of someone else, I'm well aware of its contents.
  • Any sharp or harmful articles I may have packed are not in my hand luggage but in my checked baggage.
  • I do not have any dangerous goods in my baggage
  • If I'm carrying Lithium-Ion batteries, I am not carrying more than two(2) spare batteries and they are packed in insulated packaging and placed in my cabin baggage only.
  • If I am carrying E-cigarettes, they will remain unused at all times an in my carry-on baggage only

*If any of the statements are false please check in at the kulula check-in counters.
